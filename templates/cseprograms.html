Mtech in CSE

M.Tech in Computer Science

Duration of program :2 years
Seats :24 students(First year in take)

The Department offers a post Graduate course "M Tech in Computer Science and engineering" with a specialization in the field of computer science and it's related aspects. It is a two years course divided into four semesters. Post Graduate in computer science and engineering has a wide range of job opportunities like Software engineers, application developers, network engineer, hardware engineer etc. After completion of post graduation, one may pursue Ph.D in the said subject.
